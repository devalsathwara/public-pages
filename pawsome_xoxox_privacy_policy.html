Privacy Policy • Last Updated: June 21, 2026

Welcome to Pawsome XOXOX ("we", "our", or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile application handles user information.

🛡️ 1. Zero Personal Data Collection

Pawsome XOXOX is built with your privacy in mind:

  • No Accounts: You do not need to create an account or sign up to play the game.
  • No Personal Information: We do not collect, transmit, or share any personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, location, or device identifiers.

💾 2. Local Storage Only

All game setups, custom player profiles (display names and selected avatars), and match record books are stored strictly locally on your device using a secure SQLite database and local preferences. This data never leaves your device and is not uploaded to any remote server. You can clear this data at any time by clearing the app cache/data in your device settings or via the in-app options.

🤖 3. Third-Party Services & APIs

To ensure a seamless user experience, the application utilizes standard Google Play Services for specific utility purposes:

  • Google Play In-App Updates: We use the official Google Play In-App Update API to check if a new version of the app is available and perform updates. Google Play Services may collect diagnostic information and anonymous usage statistics in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy.

👶 4. Children's Privacy

Our game is designed to be family-friendly and is suitable for children of all ages. Because we do not collect any personal data or transmit any user information, the application complies with children's privacy regulations worldwide, including the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

🔐 5. Security

Since your game records and profile preferences are stored locally on your device, the security of your data depends on the security of your physical device. We recommend keeping your device OS updated and secure.

📝 6. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be made available within the application or package updates. We encourage you to review this page periodically for any updates.
